Are you looking for a console that can entertain in a variety of ways? Do you like having plenty of games to choose from? Then the Xbox Series X might be the console you're looking for.

Being able to support 8k image quality isn't this console's only selling point. This console also lets you access XBox Game Pass. For $10 a month, you can gain access to a library of around 300 games with just that one purhcase.

If you're feeling a little nostalgic then the XBox Series X also has you covered there. The XBox Series X is one of the best consoles when it comes to backwards compatibility. The XBox Series X can play old video games from the XBox One from 2013 to the original XBox from 2001. That's around 20 years of games that can all be played on the XBox Series X.

Once you're done with games maybe you would want to relax and rewind by watching a show or film. Well, with the Xbox Series X, you don't even have to use another device. Apps like Netflix, Disney+, Hulu, and Amazon Video are all available to download on the XBox Series X. The console is a great device for entertainment.

The Xbox Series X is a great console if you want to play a lot of entertaining games and watch some entertaining shows right after. The Xbox Series X truly is a center of entertainment. You can buy it at Amazon right here or visit their website right here.
